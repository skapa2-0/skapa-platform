Frequently asked questions

How does billing work?

You pay per token: input tokens (your prompt) and output tokens (the model's response) are billed separately. Each model has its own input/output rates. Usage is tracked in real time in your dashboard.

Are there minimum charges?

No. There are no minimum spend requirements. Add credits to your account and pay only for what you use. Unused credits remain in your balance until you use them.

How do I add credits?

After signing up, go to your Dashboard → Billing. You can add credits via credit card or other supported payment methods. Credits are applied instantly to your account.

Can I set usage limits?

Yes. You can set daily or monthly spending limits in your account settings. We'll notify you when you approach your limit so you stay in control of your usage.
